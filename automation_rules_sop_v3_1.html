Automation Rules SOP v3.1

Keep automation high without letting the process become unsafe or chaotic.

Three automation modes

Autopilot Research

  • Scan sources
  • Read PDFs/RFQs
  • Extract fields
  • Score opportunities
  • Find suppliers
  • Draft packs
  • Update records

Approval-Gated Action

  • Supplier outreach
  • Buyer quote
  • Tender clarification
  • HS/origin claim
  • Payment terms
  • Document upload

Blocked Without Owner

  • Submit bid
  • Use DSC/credentials
  • Commit price
  • Pay EMD/security
  • Sign contract
  • Claim compliance as final

Fast kill rules

  • Reject deadlines under 48 hours unless owner explicitly marks urgent.
  • Reject high EMD/PBG without a capital plan.
  • Reject mandatory past-experience or turnover gaps.
  • Reject OEM lock-in unless authorized partner is available.
  • Reject fake/weak export buyers.
  • Reject restricted/prohibited/SCOMET-sensitive cases without expert partner.
  • Reject supplier search failures after minimum 5-3-2 sourcing.

Timebox rules

  • Fast Kill: 10 minutes max per opportunity.
  • Deep Read: 45 minutes max unless score above 80.
  • Supplier Search: 90 minutes max before watchlist/reject.
  • Pricing Pack: 45 minutes max after supplier proof.
  • Approval Decision: same day where possible.

Receipt rule

Every external send, owner decision, supplier quote, final classification, bid submission, buyer quote, payment commitment or case rejection must create a receipt with proof.
