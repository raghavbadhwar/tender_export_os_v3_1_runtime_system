# Bid Pack Production

Use this skill to assemble a GOV tender bid pack draft.

## Inputs
- deep read report
- pricing proof
- supplier shortlist
- compliance/eligibility matrix
- approval policy

## Pack Includes
- bid cover page
- BOQ with quantities and draft prices
- compliance matrix
- eligibility declaration draft
- supplier summary
- EMD/security plan
- timeline and delivery plan
- risk register
- missing items list
- approval card if external submission is proposed

## Validation
- `case_id` appears in every artifact.
- Missing items are explicit.
- No unread clause is treated as complied with.
- No final price or submission is committed without owner approval.

## Must Not
- submit bid
- upload documents
- use DSC
- pay EMD/security
- claim eligibility without evidence
