# Export Compliance Review

Use this skill for export RFQ compliance drafts.

## Boundary
AI may suggest candidate classifications. Human/export expert approval is required for final HSN/ITC-HS, origin, SCOMET, FTA, or compliance claims.

## Outputs
- draft HSN/ITC-HS candidate
- export policy condition
- restricted/prohibited/SCOMET flag
- destination-country requirements
- SPS/TBT alerts where relevant
- certificate of origin requirements
- rules of origin analysis
- FTA/preferential tariff possibility
- Incoterms recommendation
- IEC/GST/e-RCMC checklist
- packing list/proforma/commercial invoice templates
- labelling, packaging, freight, and insurance assumptions

## Stop Conditions
- SCOMET item suspected
- prohibited category
- source evidence is not available
- classification ambiguity is material

## Must Not
- confirm HSN/ITC-HS
- claim origin
- guarantee compliance
- send export quotation

## Sources
Cite DGFT/export policy pages, tariff references, RFQ documents, and local case files used.
