# ChatGPT Boardroom Handoff

Use this skill when preparing a bounded research or strategy packet for ChatGPT Project.

## Inputs
- `data/chatgpt_snapshot.md`
- selected case reports or summaries
- specific research question
- source health and plugin health summaries

## Procedure
1. Generate or refresh `data/chatgpt_snapshot.md`.
2. Ask one bounded strategy or research question.
3. Include known evidence and known gaps.
4. Exclude raw operational database dumps.
5. Save returned research to Drive and link it to cases/tasks.

## Must Not
- send credentials, DSC, bank details, tokens, or raw supplier tables
- ask ChatGPT to submit, send, pay, or approve
- treat ChatGPT as final legal/compliance authority
